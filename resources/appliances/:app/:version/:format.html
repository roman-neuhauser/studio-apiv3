Methods

DELETE

Delete the built :format image of the :version of :app.

Spec:

Accept:
  $CT:result

Status:
  200 Ok
| 409 Conflict
| 412 Precondition Failed
Content-Type:
  &Accept

Example:

< DELETE /appliances/123/4.5.6/vmx

> 204 No Content
